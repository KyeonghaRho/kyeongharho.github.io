About me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at KAIST, advised by Professor Joon Son Chung. I earned my M.S. supervised by Professor Joon Son Chung at KAIST. My research aims to learn semantically rich representations from multiple modalities (including vision, text and audio) and harness them to solve multimodal downstream tasks.
